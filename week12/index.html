Milti-channel controller

I want to use a Mini 2-Axis Analog Thumbstick to control the position of a circle instead of a potentiometer. I thought it would look really cool if the circle could follow the direction of the thumbstick and move around the screen.

While building the circuit, I realized how crowded everything became, I definitely need a longer breadboard! It took some time to arrange the components, and the thumbstick legs didn’t fit the breadboard very well, so they kept falling out.

The potentiometer (which controls the circle’s size) and the button (which changes the background color) work perfectly, but the thumbstick does not. Right now, I can only move the circle up and down, not left and right, don’t even mention let it fully follow the thumbstick’s movement.
